# Sales and Profit Performance Analysis (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes sales and profit performance across products, regions, and customer segments using **Power BI**. The objective is to identify key revenue drivers, uncover loss areas, and provide actionable business recommendations based on data insights.

The analysis focuses on understanding overall business performance, profitability trends, and customer behavior to support better decision-making.

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI  
- Power Query (Data Cleaning and Transformation)  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

---

## Data Cleaning and Transformation
The dataset was cleaned and prepared to ensure accuracy and consistency before analysis. Key steps include:

- Calculated total loss, total profit, selling price, and net profit  
- Checked for and removed duplicate records  
- Standardized date formats  
- Cleaned and renamed columns for clarity  
- Extracted year, month name, day name, and week type for time-based analysis  

---

## DAX Measures Created
The following DAX measures were used to support analysis and performance tracking:

- Average profit per customer  
- Average sales per customer  
- Total loss  
- Net profit  
- Profit margin percentage  
- Total sales  
- Total profits  
- Total customers  
- Total cities  
- Total countries  
- Total regions  

---

## Key Insights
- Overall performance shows steady growth in both sales and profit.  
- Technology products generate the highest revenue and profitability, while Furniture records losses mainly due to heavy discounting.  
- Western Europe is the strongest-performing region, leading in both sales and profit.  
- The Consumer segment contributes the highest share of revenue, indicating strong customer loyalty and repeat purchase behavior.  

---

## Recommendations
- Increase focus on Technology products to sustain and improve profit growth.  
- Review Furniture pricing and discount strategies to reduce losses and improve margins.  
- Expand marketing efforts in emerging regions with growth potential.  
- Strengthen customer loyalty initiatives for the Consumer segment.  
- Monitor sales and profit trends quarterly to maintain performance consistency.  

---

## Outcome
This project demonstrates the use of Power BI for transforming raw sales data into meaningful insights and business recommendations that support strategic planning and performance improvement.
