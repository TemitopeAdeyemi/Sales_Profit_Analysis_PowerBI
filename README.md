##  Data Cleaning and Transformation
The dataset was cleaned and prepared to ensure accuracy and consistency prior to analysis. Key steps include:

- Calculated total loss, total profit, selling price, and net profit  
- Checked for and removed duplicate records  
- Standardized date formats  
- Cleaned and renamed columns for clarity and consistency  
- Extracted year, month name, day name, and week type to support time-based analysis  

---

##  DAX Measures Created

- Average profit per customer  
- Average sales per customer  
- Total loss  
- Net profit  
- Profit margin percentage  
- Total sales  
- Total profits  
- Total customers  
- Total cities  
- Total countries  
- Total regions
